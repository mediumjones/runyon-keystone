01Identity Monitoring

Identity Monitoring is Project Keystone's unified surveillance layer, bundling six previously fragmented protection capabilities into a single, always-on service. It acts as the product's “eyes,” continuously watching a member's identity, credit, financial accounts, and behavioral patterns for early signs of compromise, fraud, or exploitation.

End of section 01

02Activity stream

A single chronological feed of every signal surfaced — from dark web hits and breach notifications to credit changes and unusual account activity, each tagged with its source and severity. It gives members one place to see everything being watched on their behalf, so nothing gets lost across the six underlying monitors and users always have a clear answer to “what's happened recently?”

End of section 02

03Financial Account(s) Monitoring

The most NerdWallet-branded screen in the prototype suite. Tests whether making NerdWallet’s brand presence more prominent increases perceived trust and value. This sub-page of Finances shows cross-account behavioral AI monitoring: a single view of all connected financial accounts with AI-powered transaction analysis. The key variable under test is NerdWallet brand integration.
